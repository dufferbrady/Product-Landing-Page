Premium Car Washes

Water Purity

  • Our water is treated with a highly advanced water purification system called reverse osmosis or RO.
  • RO uses a semipermeable membrane and extremly high pressures to cleanse the water of ions, molecules and other larger particles from the water.

Wheel Rim Wash

  • All tyres are washed thoroughly by our trained wash attendants before entering the wash tunnel.
  • Once inside the tunnel each wheel is detected and scrubbed by our advanced brush sensor technology.
  • Our wash can also dry each wheel which keeps your alloys clean post wash.

Quality Assurance

  • All products we use on your vehicle are 100% quality assured and safe to use on all vehicle finishes.
  • We use a high foaming wash and wax that gets your car sparkling clean and leave an invisble layer of protection for a superior shine
  • Our water is so clean and pure that it can be considered drinking water due to our RO system.

Wash Tunnel

  • Our state of the art wash tunnel is over 100 feet long ensuring plenty of time to get your vehicle sparkling clean.
  • Our tunnel only takes 7 minutes to get from start to finish ensuring both quality and speed.
  • Our wash tunnel uses conveyor belt technology that pulls each vehicle along the way so you can relax while the wash does its job.
